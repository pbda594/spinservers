## What Makes SpinServers Different

The thing that stands out immediately is how they keep prices low. SpinServers is vertically integrated with TheServerStore.com — one of the largest traders of used enterprise server hardware globally, operating a 40,000-square-foot warehouse in Texas. That supply chain advantage means they can put Dell, HP, Quanta, and Supermicro gear in front of you at prices that feel slightly unreal.

A few things are non-negotiable in their setup regardless of which plan you pick:

- **Dual 10Gbps ports** — not an add-on, just the baseline
- **Month-to-month billing** — no annual contracts, no setup fees
- **30-minute provisioning** on instant inventory
- **Free Virtual Private Cloud (VPC)** — build private L2 networks between your servers at no extra cost
- **IPMI/iKVM access** — full console access even if you brick the OS
- **SOC2, HIPAA, and PCI compliant** Tier III datacenters

They operate their own ASN (AS 396073) with a multihomed network connecting Cogent, HE, Level 3, IX, and others. Palo Alto carrier-grade firewalls and DDoS protection are part of the infrastructure.
